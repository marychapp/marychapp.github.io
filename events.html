MARY CHAPP Design & Illustration

Campus Events Handout

Unigraphics, my old place of employment, often catered to Bowling Green State University, one of their top clients. Despite having no affiliation with the university, Unigraphics chose its name to show that it supported the campus and its happenings. Many of the projects we designed for the university were for events so that people who were entering college as freshmen were given information on how to become more involved in campus life as well as their community. Pictured are the front and back of a small postcard sized handout we gave to all of the freshmen at their orientation night. Although colors and fonts were already designated before we began the designs, examples like these were great learning tools for me on how to present information cohesively and clearly using hierarchy, color and layout.
