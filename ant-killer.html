MARY CHAPP Design & Illustration

Ant Killer

I love books. Reading, writing, designing and binding them. For several years, I considered becoming a writer. In the end, art won me over. However, writing still plays an important role in my design work. I've taken book design and book binding courses in college. A good friend of mine, Suzanna Anderson, a creative writing major, has seen her work published in several magazines. A collection of short stories was penned and headed under her most popular work, “The Ant Killer.” I saw it as a fantastic opportunity to design a layout for the cover of her book. Like a classic murder novel, a pool of blood spreads across the cover, made up of swarming red ants. Anyone who finds creepy crawlys unsettling will have a hard time picking this book up, but it's well worth it.
